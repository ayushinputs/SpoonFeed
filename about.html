About SpoonFeed

We're on a mission to revolutionize the restaurant industry with cutting-edge technology that empowers businesses and delights customers.

Our Origin Story

Founded in 2024 in the heart of Bilaspur, SpoonFeed was born from a simple observation: restaurants were struggling with outdated systems while customers craved seamless digital experiences.

Our founders, having witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by local restaurants, decided to bridge this gap by bringing big-tech solutions to every dining establishment, regardless of size or budget.
